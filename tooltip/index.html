Jordan Barone
Neuroscience and Child Development, Minor in French

Campus Activities: Tour Guides, Figure Skating Club, NeuroMajors Club

Most Outstanding Vanderbilt Memory: This is impossible to narrow down, but one of my favorite memories while at Vanderbilt was when the Figure Skating Club hosted our first-ever event. A friend and I had started the club during my sophomore year, and although we had a small group of skaters practicing weekly, we wanted to do something that could involve our other friends and the rest of the campus. I was so nervous that we wouldn't be able to rent the rink, that people wouldn't come, or that no one would have fun, but we ended up getting over 50 people to come out to Centennial Sportsplex for a public skate session on a Saturday afternoon last fall. We had food, music, and so many more attendees than we ever thought possible, and it was an amazing realization of how supportive the Vanderbilt community is and how many people were willing to try something new.
